1. Introduction

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you fill out contact forms, call us, email us, communicate with us, or request help. This may include your name, email address, phone number, property location or service area, property type, preferred contact method, urgency, and any other information you choose to provide.

Because our website helps route mold-related inquiries, you may also provide details about the affected room or building, visible growth, odor, moisture, water damage history, HVAC concerns, photos, reports, tenant or business concerns, and the type of help that appears closest to your situation.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect information about your device and browsing behavior, including IP address, approximate location data, browser type and version, operating system, pages visited, time spent on pages, referring website addresses, device information, and interaction data.

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies, web beacons, pixels, local storage, and similar technologies to support website functionality, remember preferences, understand site performance, and evaluate inquiry or advertising performance. For more details, please see our Cookie Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to receive and respond to inquiries, communicate with you, help route your request to the right type of mold-related support, and connect you with participating independent local professionals where available.

We may also use information for website analytics, service-area operations, customer support, fraud prevention, security, legal compliance, recordkeeping, business operations, and improving how our website explains inspection, remediation, emergency response, HVAC/odor, commercial, and prevention services.

Where required by law, we will obtain consent before using your information for certain marketing or advertising purposes.
