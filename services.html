Choosing a path

How do you know where to begin?

Mold concerns can overlap. The best first step usually depends on what you can see, what you can smell, whether anything is still wet, and whether people are actively using the space.

You can see growth

Home or commercial cleanup may be the right path, with inspection added if the source or affected area is unclear.

You smell something musty

Inspection, air review, HVAC checks, or moisture assessment may help before assuming what needs to be removed.

Something recently got wet

Urgent drying, inspection, or prevention may matter first because wet materials can become a mold risk if they stay damp.
